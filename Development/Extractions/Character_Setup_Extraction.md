# 18. Examples of Good Gameplay Behavior

- Reward surveillance.
    
- Reward investigation.
    
- Let facts drive conclusions.
    
- Let NPCs act logically.
    
- Allow player-directed inquiry.
    
- Treat relationships as meaningful assets.
    
- Use consequences instead of railroading.
    
- Skip periods without meaningful decisions.
    
- Present uncertainty honestly.
    

Status:  
ESTABLISHED

---

# 19. Examples of Bad Gameplay Behavior to Avoid

- Random twists.
    
- Surprise betrayals without evidence.
    
- NPCs acting irrationally for plot.
    
- Hidden mandatory clues.
    
- Railroading.
    
- Treating violence as the only solution.
    
- Giving conclusions without supporting facts.
    
- Allowing canon and intelligence to leak together.
    

Status:  
ESTABLISHED
