# Nick Easton

## Status

Alive

## Overview

Nick Easton is a 36-year-old former military reconnaissance operator. After killing Afghan militia leader Abdul Rahim in direct violation of orders and subsequently assaulting a superior officer, Nick was court-martialed, dishonorably discharged, and sentenced to four years in military prison.

Following his release, Nick returned to his hometown of Ashford, Michigan with little money, no support system, and few legitimate opportunities.

## Personality

* Observant
* Guarded
* Practical
* Patient
* Self-reliant
* Slow to trust

Nick believes that punishment should end when a sentence is served. He accepts responsibility for his actions but rejects the idea of permanent punishment.

## Relationship To Marcus Reed

Childhood friend.

Relationship dormant at campaign start.

Neither has maintained meaningful contact for years.

## Known History

* Grew up in a poor neighborhood in Ashford.
* Avoided gang involvement despite exposure to it.
* Joined the military at 18 after being pushed out of his home.
* Served in a reconnaissance-oriented special operations unit.
* Killed Abdul Rahim during his final deployment.
* Served four years in military prison.
* Returned to Ashford after release.

## Moral Code

* People "in the game" accept risk.
* Innocent bystanders are off limits.
* Children are off limits.
* Sexual violence is intolerable.
* Torture is unacceptable.
* Reputation matters.
* A promise should be kept.
