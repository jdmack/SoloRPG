# Ashford, Michigan

## Overview

Ashford is a fictional Michigan city serving as the primary setting of the campaign.

Population is approximately 250,000.

## Description

Former manufacturing city.

Experienced economic decline after the loss of major industry.

Never fully collapsed, but never fully recovered.

Contains:

* Working-class neighborhoods
* Industrial districts
* Revitalized downtown areas
* Wealthier suburbs
* Criminal activity
* Political corruption

## Northside

Working-class neighborhood where Nick and Marcus grew up.

Originally a respectable community.

Declined over several decades.

Contains abandoned properties, struggling businesses, and long-time residents.

## Themes

Ashford serves as a reflection of Nick himself:

* Once full of promise
* Scarred by hardship
* Still functioning despite damage
