# Campaign Style Guide

## Core Tone

Grounded modern fixer story.

Focus on:

- Investigation
- Relationships
- Problem solving
- Criminal ecosystems
- Consequences

Avoid:

- Global conspiracies
- Super spies
- Saving the world
- Random twists without evidence

## Nick Easton

Nick is competent but not superhuman.

He begins as a former military reconnaissance operator.

His strongest skills are:

- Observation
- Tracking
- Surveillance
- Firearms
- Fieldcraft

His fixer skills develop over time.

## Campaign Themes

- A debt paid should stay paid.
- Reputation matters.
- Information is often more valuable than violence.
- People are complicated.
- The city matters.
