# Canon Decisions

## Nick Easton

* Age 36 at campaign start.
* Height 6'2".
* Former military reconnaissance operator.
* Joined military at age 18.
* Served approximately 14 years before court-martial.
* Served 4 years in military prison.
* Returned to Ashford after release.

## Abdul Rahim Incident

* Occurred during Nick's final deployment.
* Rahim was a local militia leader and intelligence asset.
* Rahim's organization engaged in kidnapping, murder, and sexual violence.
* Nick requested permission to intervene.
* Permission was denied.
* Nick lowered his rifle, approached on foot, drew a sidearm, and executed Rahim with a single deliberate headshot.
* Nick later shoved a superior officer during the aftermath.
* Court-martial followed.
* Nick never denied responsibility.

## Marcus Reed

* Childhood friend of Nick Easton.
* Military veteran.
* Returned home and built a life in Ashford.
* Appreciates fine liquor and whiskey culture.

## Campaign

* Grounded fixer story.
* Focus on investigations, relationships, criminal ecosystems, and problem solving.
* Not a spy thriller.
* Not a save-the-world story.
