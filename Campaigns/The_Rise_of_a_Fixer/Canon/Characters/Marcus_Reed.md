# Marcus Reed

## Status

Alive

## Overview

Marcus Reed is a childhood friend of Nick Easton.

Unlike Nick, Marcus returned home and successfully built a life within Ashford's gray economy. He serves as a connector between legitimate businesses, blue-collar workers, local operators, and criminal-adjacent circles.

## Appearance

* Age: 38
* Height: 6'0"
* Build: Approximately 225 lbs
* African-American
* Trimmed beard
* Maintains a professional but casual appearance

## Personality

* Charismatic
* Connected
* Loyal
* Practical
* Social

Marcus enjoys good liquor and has turned drinking into a hobby and area of expertise rather than simple recreation.

Marcus prefers bourbon, rye, and scotch.

He enjoys discussing distilleries, production methods, and history.

His interest in liquor evolved from youthful partying into a genuine hobby.

## Relationship To Nick

Childhood friend.

Initially hesitant to associate publicly with Nick due to reputational concerns.

Eventually becomes one of Nick's earliest and most important allies.

## Known History

* Grew up in the same neighborhood as Nick.
* Served in the military.
* Returned home and built a network of contacts.
* Known for connecting people and solving local problems.

## Notes

Marcus is one of the few people who remembers who Nick was before the military, prison, and fixer life.
